How to Apply?

  3. Make the payment securely through our portal and Receive your policy details and enjoy coverage

Claims

Medical insurance claims allow policyholders to get financial support for medical expenses. Choose between cashless and reimbursement claims.

Types of Claims

  • Cashless Claims: Get treatment at network hospitals without upfront payment.
  • Reimbursement Claims: Pay medical bills first and submit for reimbursement.

Claim Submission Process

  1. Fill out the claim form.
  2. Upload necessary documents (bills, prescriptions, reports).
  3. Submit the claim for processing.
  4. Track your claim status online.

Claim Tracking

Enter your Claim ID or Policy Number to check claim status.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What documents are required for medical insurance?

A: You need identity proof, address proof, and medical history documents.

Q: How long does it take to process claims?

A: Cashless claims are processed within 24 hours, while reimbursement claims take 7-10 days.

Q: Can I include my parents in the policy?

A: Yes, you can opt for a family plan that includes parents.
