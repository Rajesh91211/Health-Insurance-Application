Terms and Conditions

1. Policy Coverage

Coverage begins 24 hours after policy issuance for accidents and after specified waiting periods for illnesses. Pre-existing conditions have a waiting period of 24 months.

2. Premium Payments

Premiums must be paid annually in advance. A grace period of 30 days is allowed for renewal payments. Non-payment results in policy lapse.

3. Claims Process

Claims must be filed within 30 days of treatment. All supporting documents must be original or certified copies. False claims will result in policy termination.

4. Policy Renewal

Policies are renewable annually subject to continuous coverage. Premium rates may be revised based on claim history and age.

5. Cancellation

The company reserves the right to cancel policies in cases of misrepresentation or fraud. Refunds will be processed as per regulatory guidelines.
